Morteza Ghahremani Ph.D.

AI/ML Scientist at Technical University of Munich (TUM) & Munich Center for Machine Learning (MCML) & Novarc Technologies Inc.

Welcome to the innovative universe of Mori! As a member of the AI-Med group at TUM, Mori is at the cutting edge of AI research, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the intersection of computer vision and medical imaging. Mori is also operating the GenAI pipeline at Novarc Technologies Inc., where generative models dynamically enhance the quality of downstream tasks such as segmentation.

When Mori isn't buried in tasks or tinkering with algorithms, you'll find a playful spirit who loves to explore the intersection of AI and creativity, whether it's generating quirky AI-generated art, imagining whimsical future tech, or just having a good laugh at the latest AI memes.
